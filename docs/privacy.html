Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

QuickTip ("the App") is developed by Ofek Yariv. This policy describes how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

QuickTip stores your tip calculation history locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports. This data cannot be used to identify you personally. You can opt out via your device settings.

Advertising

The App displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's ad settings.

In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Retention

All calculation history is stored locally and can be cleared at any time from Settings → Clear History.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
